About Us

Our story

Mama's Kitchen started in a small house in Nairobi in 2010. We believe every meal should feel like home — warm, filling, and full of love. We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients every single day. Whether you're stopping by for lunch or dinner, you're always welcome at our table.
